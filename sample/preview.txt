🎨 COLOR PALETTE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Primary Blue:    #2c3e50  (dark blue-gray for headings)
Accent Orange:   #e67e22  (highlights, borders, decorations)
Background:      #fdf6e3  (warm cream/parchment)
Text:            #34495e  (dark gray for readability)
Code Background: #faf8f0  (light cream for code blocks)

🔤 TYPOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Code:        JetBrains Mono - professional monospace
