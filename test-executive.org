** Coffee Recipe (V60)

> ⭐ **Rating:** ★★★★★  |  ⏱️ **Time:** 5 min  |  #**ID:** v60-recipe

Brewing the perfect cup of coffee using the V60 method.

*** Ingredients

- 20g freshly ground coffee beans
- 300ml water at 96°C
- V60 dripper and filter

*** Steps

1. Boil water to 96°C
2. Rinse filter with hot water
3. Add medium-fine ground coffee
4. Pour 60ml water in circular motion (bloom for 30s)
5. Slowly pour remaining water in circles
6. Total brew time: 2:30-3:00 minutes
